Instructions

'Concentration', also known as 'Match Match', 'Memory', 'Pelmanism', 'Shinkei-suijaku', 'Pexeso', 'Pairs', or in our case, 'Super Memory', is a card game in which all of the cards are laid face-down on a surface and two cards are flipped face-up over each turn. The object of the game is to turn over pairs of matching cards. Simple enough? Then let's dive in!
